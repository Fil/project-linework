=============================================
Angular: A linework set from Project Linework
=============================================

Traced and expanded by Dennis McClendon.

Derived from a “corporate annual report” which Dennis McClendon “happened upon circa 1983,” and which he maintained in his inspiration file for many years. The Canadian provinces are his own addition.
